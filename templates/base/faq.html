BUYERS

There is a secure purchase system on SALT. That means that the seller receives payment only after the buyer confirms the successful delivery of the item. I want to buy an item on SALT. Hmm ... How do I do this? - Choose on the item you want to purchase and click on BUY SECURE - Enter your details and select the address of the nearest office of Nova Poshta - Check the cost of goods and delivery, then click on PAY - On the LiqPay website, enter the card details safely (PrivatBank, Visa, MasterCard), and that's it - the item was bought! Payment completed. What's next? - The seller receives a notification of the purchase and shippment data for sending the item - Once the item has been sent, in the ORDERS section you can track delivery by the tracking number - When the item is delivered, confirm the delivery in the ORDERS section. When am I gonna receive the item? I can't wait! - For the residents of Ukraine, delivery is carried out through Nova Posta - The exact delivery time depends on the regions of the sender and recipient. Usually it is 1-2 days from the moment of sending - You can calculate delivery times here: https://novaposhta.ua/en/onlineorder/estimatedate Is SALT free? - Yes, shopping on SALT is absolutely free. And also - 100%% safe I received the item, but ... it does not match the description. What do I do? - In the ORDERS section, click on REPORT and we will contact you within 24 hours to double-check - If the item, indeed, does not match the description, we will refund you money on the specified card
